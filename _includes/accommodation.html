Visa Information

Citizens from the following countries are exempted from visa requirements for short stays in Ghana: ECOWAS countries (14), Rwanda, Kenya, Lesotho, Namibia, Singapore, Tanzania, Trinidad and Tobago, Uganda, Zimbabwe, Seychelles, Mauritius, Malawi. If your country is not on the list above, please contact the Ghanaian Embassy or High Commission in your country to check whether you need a visa to visit Ghana.
